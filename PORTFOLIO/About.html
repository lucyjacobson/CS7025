Hey everyone, I’m Lucy – nice to meet y’all!

I was born in Nashville, Tennessee, and have been on the move along the East Coast and abroad ever since. At age 7, I drove my Barbie Corvette off a ramp, and my love for risk-taking was born (though Mom and Dad weren’t thrilled). Today, that same adventurous spirit shapes my approach to design – I like to push the boundaries with color and typography. Taking an idea from paper to screen to create digital experiences people genuinely enjoy is what keeps me going. When I’m not clicking around on my computer, I’m usually hanging out with my dog, Oliver, my pride and joy, and best friend with a consistent width from head to tail. You might also find me writing or sharing movie reviews on my Instagram at @lucygoestothemovies.
